How to play Drawry

1. All players title a storybook, and write the first page

2. The books are shuffled and prompts drawn by random players

3. Then the drawing is passed to another random player to describe

4. Steps 2 and 3 repeat until all the books are finished!

5. At the end, each player takes turns presenting the storybook they started to everyone else!!!
